About SRI Academy

Discover our legacy of excellence in education and commitment to holistic development

Our Story

A journey of nurturing young minds since our founding

SRI Academy was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education that blends traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. Over the past four decades, we have grown into a leading educational institution in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, known for our commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

Our campus, nestled in the heart of the city, is a vibrant community where students, teachers, and parents collaborate to create a nurturing environment. We believe in empowering our students to become confident, compassionate, and capable leaders of tomorrow.

From humble beginnings with a single classroom to a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art facilities, SRI Academy continues to innovate and inspire, staying true to our core values of integrity, respect, and excellence.
