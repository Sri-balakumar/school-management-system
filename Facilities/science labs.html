Science Labs

At SRI Academy, our state-of-the-art Science Labs provide students with hands-on opportunities to explore the wonders of physics, chemistry, and biology. Equipped with modern tools and safety features, these labs foster curiosity, critical thinking, and scientific discovery in a controlled and inspiring environment.

  • Advanced equipment for experiments in physics, chemistry, and biology.
  • Safety systems including fume hoods, emergency showers, and fire extinguishers.
  • Dedicated lab stations for collaborative and individual experiments.
  • Digital tools for data analysis and experiment simulation.
  • Regularly updated resources to align—with current scientific standards.
